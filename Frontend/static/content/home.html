What is RetroPong?

RetroPong is a modern take on the classic game of Pong. The game is played by two players, each controlling a paddle on either side of the screen. The game is won when one player scores 10 points.

Features

Unique Gameplay

RetroPong provides a unique game play experience merging between the cool vapor wave aesthetic and the nostalgic feeling of playing pong in a single package!

Tournaments

Engage in fulfilling tournaments with other players and compete to be the best. Join a team and compete in the leaderboards now!

Pong with Powerups

RetroPong has multiple kinds of powerups that can be used to gain an advantage over your opponent! Use them wisely to win the game!
